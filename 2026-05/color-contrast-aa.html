Color Contrast AA Test

WCAG Success Criterion tested: 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum)

This success criterion requires text and images of text to meet minimum contrast requirements so they are readable for people with low vision or reduced contrast sensitivity. For normal-sized text, WCAG AA requires a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1.
